Nool of the Month

Norris Porris

Description

Hailing from the small forest region of Floridas, Norris Porris, was born a hobgoblin. He was, however, banished from his homeland for unspeakable crimes and cursed to dwell on the mountais of Hamburrough.

This, unfortunately, only increased his mischievous ways and now he is a wanted man throughout the Capital.

Wanted For

  • Moral Indecency
  • Practicing the Arkane Arts
  • Gluttony
  • Breaking the Faerie Covenant
  • Blapping His Majesty, King Cyril II
